Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access support services?

You can reach our helpline 24/7 at 1-800-CARE-123 or use the contact form. We're here to listen and connect you with resources tailored to your needs.

Are the resources free to use?

Yes! All our support services and educational resources are completely free. We believe everyone should have access to quality care information and emotional support.

Can I volunteer with your organization?

Absolutely. We welcome volunteers in many capacities. Contact us to learn about volunteer opportunities that match your skills and availability.

How is my privacy protected?

Your privacy is our priority. All conversations are confidential and handled with care. We never share personal information without consent.
